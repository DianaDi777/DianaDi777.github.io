--QUOTES--

Use :
- class "blockquate" for parent element (< blockquote >) to style quote the way presented below.
- class "blockquate-p" for child elemepnt (paragraph < p > )
- class "blockquate-c" for child elemepnt (< cite > )
- add desired typeface by adding one of the typefaces classses (check the TYPE_FACE section).
In example below we used class="helvetica" for paragraph < p >

‘Life’s but a walking shadow, a poor player, that struts and frets his hour upon the stage, and then is heard no more; it is a tale told by an idiot, full of sound and fury, signifying nothing.’

―William Shakespeare 

      <blockquote class="helvetica blockquate">
        <p class="blockquate-p">
         ‘Life’s but a walking shadow, a poor player, that struts and frets his
         hour upon the stage, and then is heard no more; it is a tale told by
         an idiot, full of sound and fury, signifying nothing.’
        </p>
        <cite class="blockquate-c">―William Shakespeare </cite>
      </blockquote>
